How it works

From data audit to live dashboard in 5 steps

A clear process — you always know what's happening and what's next.

01
Day 1 — 30 min call
Data audit
We map your existing data sources, what metrics you currently track, and what decisions you wish you could make faster. Output: dashboard blueprint + data connection plan.
02
Days 2–4
Data model design
We design the data structure, connection logic, and update frequency before building anything — so there are no surprises or rewrites later.
03
Days 4–7
Dashboard UI design
High-fidelity Figma mockup of every view in your dashboard. You review, give feedback, and approve before we write a single line of code.
04
Days 7–14
Development & integration
We build the dashboard and connect it to your live data sources. You can preview on a staging URL and track progress throughout — no black box.
05
Day 15
Training & handover
We walk your team through the dashboard, deliver full documentation, and hand over everything — source code, data pipeline access, and all credentials.
