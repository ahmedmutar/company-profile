Use Cases

What your business can stop doing manually

If it's repetitive, rule-based, and happens more than once a week — it can almost certainly be automated.

Document Processing
Invoices, purchase orders, contracts, and receipts — AI reads, extracts the right fields, and files them automatically with no human touch.
Data Entry & Sync
Data from emails, forms, spreadsheets, and scanned documents automatically enters your ERP, CRM, or database — accurate and in real time.
Approval Workflows
Leave requests, purchase approvals, expense claims — automatically routed to the right person, with escalation rules if no response within SLA.
Report Generation
Daily sales summaries, weekly inventory snapshots, monthly P&L reports — generated and sent to your inbox automatically at a schedule you define.
Smart Email Dispatch
Order confirmations, payment reminders, onboarding sequences, and customer alerts — triggered automatically by specific events with zero manual sending.
Price & Inventory Updates
Product prices, stock levels, and catalog data synced across all your sales channels — marketplace, website, and POS — without manual copy-paste.
Claim & Form Processing
Insurance claims, reimbursement forms, and customer surveys read, classified, and routed automatically — cutting processing time from days to minutes.
HR & Payroll Triggers
Attendance data, overtime hours, and allowance calculations automatically compiled and handed to payroll — no more end-of-month data scrambling.

How It Works

From kickoff to live automation in 4 steps

01
Week 1
Process audit
We spend time with your team to map every step of the target workflow — inputs, decision points, outputs, exceptions. We also identify which parts are genuinely automatable and which still need human judgment.
02
Week 1–2
Build & train
We build the automation logic and train the AI models on your actual documents and data. Integrations to your existing systems are tested in a staging environment before anything touches production.
03
Week 2–3
Parallel run & validation
The automation runs alongside your manual process for one to two weeks. We compare outputs, measure accuracy, and fix edge cases before flipping the switch fully. This is where confidence is built.
04
Week 3–4
Go live + handover
The automation goes fully live. Your team gets trained on the monitoring dashboard. We stay on for 3 months to track performance, retrain models as your data evolves, and expand coverage if needed.
