A corner of calm in the middle of the city

NOOK started with a simple idea: a café that doesn't try too hard. No gimmicks, no theater — just genuinely good food, coffee sourced from farms we trust, and a room that feels like it was designed for lingering.

Every dish on our menu went through rounds of testing before it made the cut. The truffle pasta took eleven versions. The cold brew recipe hasn't changed since day one because it's never needed to.

We're in South Jakarta, open every day, and we mean it when we say you're welcome to stay as long as you like.
