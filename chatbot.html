Use Cases

What your chatbot can handle

If it's a question your team answers more than twice a week, the chatbot can handle it.

Customer Service & FAQ
Answers product questions, pricing, operating hours, shipping policies, and return procedures — instantly, in the customer's own language.
Lead Qualification
Asks the right questions, scores the lead, collects contact details, and passes warm prospects directly to your sales team — ready to close.
Appointment Booking
Lets customers book consultations, demo calls, or service slots directly in the chat — synced to your calendar with automatic reminders.
Order Status & Tracking
Customers type an order number and get live status updates pulled from your system — no agent needed, no queue to wait in.
Internal HR Bot
Employees ask about leave balances, payslips, company policies, and onboarding steps — answered instantly without emailing HR.
After-Hours Support
When your team is offline, the chatbot collects issue details and contact information so agents can follow up first thing in the morning with full context.
Product Recommendations
Asks a few questions about the customer's need and suggests the right product or package from your catalogue — like a trained sales rep, always available.
Document & Form Collection
Guides customers through submitting required documents, filling forms, or completing onboarding steps — reducing incomplete submissions.

How It Works

From kickoff to live chatbot in 3 weeks

01
Week 1
Discovery & data collection
We map your most common customer questions, collect your knowledge sources (FAQs, product sheets, policy docs, past chat logs), and define the conversations the bot needs to handle well. This determines what it can and can't do at launch.
02
Week 1–2
Build & train
We structure the knowledge base, configure the LLM, set up conversation flows, define escalation triggers, and connect to your channels. The chatbot is tested internally against hundreds of real question variations before you see it.
03
Week 2–3
QA & refinement
You and your team test the chatbot with real scenarios. We fix incorrect answers, improve response quality on edge cases, and tune the handoff logic until you're confident in what it does — and what it correctly declines to answer.
04
Week 3
Go live + handover
The chatbot goes live on all configured channels. Your team gets access to the analytics dashboard and a simple content editor to add new FAQs without touching code. We monitor the first two weeks closely and handle any issues.
