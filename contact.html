FAQ

Before reaching out,
this might help

The most common questions we get from prospective clients.

Is the first consultation really free?
Yes, 100% free with no commitment. The first consultation session is usually 30–45 minutes. There's no pressure to continue to a paid project.
How long from consultation to project start?
After the initial consultation, we need 3–5 business days to prepare a proposal. Once approved, the project can start within 1–2 weeks. An MVP can be up and running in 4–6 weeks.
Is there a long-term contract lock-in?
No forced lock-in. Every project has a clear scope and duration from the start. Ongoing services can be cancelled at any time with 30 days' notice.
Do you serve clients outside Jakarta?
Yes. We've worked with clients from Surabaya, Medan, Makassar, Bali, and other cities. Almost all work can be done remotely. For physical installations, we're ready to come to your location.
